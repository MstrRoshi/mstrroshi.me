Welcome to GetGrounded!

The purpose of this application is to help users deal with stress in their daily life. Every person experiences some type of stress throughout their lives, yet there are so few people gathering together to find was to handle it. GetGrounded is designed to be your every stress relieving resource. Sort through user confirmed lists of activities from local businesses in your area offering stress relieving services, build paths through local nature trails, see what worked for similar users and share your ideas for activities with your community. Its about time we banded together against feeling awful from stress and anxiety!

Features:

  • User generated and rated lists of activities
  • GPS Mapping feature for mapping out trails, paths in your area
  • Ranking system - the more you share/rate activities and the application, the higher chance of you receiving promotional offers from our sponsors
  • Stress Quiz to help determine what kind of stress you are under
  • Localized professional help for those who feel overwhelmed
  • At home techniques to help ease stress through meditation, free yoga tutorials, guided meditation tracks, and much more!
